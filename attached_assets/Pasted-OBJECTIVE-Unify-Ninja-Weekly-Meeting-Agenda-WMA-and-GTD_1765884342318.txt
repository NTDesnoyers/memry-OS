OBJECTIVE
Unify Ninja Weekly Meeting Agenda (WMA) and GTD Weekly Review into a single weekly ritual called Weekly Operating Review (Ninja + GTD) that preserves Ninja execution integrity and GTD system trust, without duplication or task-level detail.

⸻

HARD CONSTRAINTS
	•	Weekly review must take ≤10 minutes
	•	No task management inside this app
	•	Todoist remains the task system of record
	•	This app confirms system health, execution, and results
	•	GTD steps are binary confirmations, not journaling
	•	Ninja habits remain rated and countable
	•	One row = one calendar week

⸻

MODULE NAME

Weekly Operating Review (Ninja + GTD)

⸻

SECTION 1 — WEEKLY SNAPSHOT (REQUIRED)

Fields:
	•	week_ending_date (date)
	•	business_direction_rating (int 1–10)
	•	time_energy_rating (int 1–10)
	•	ninja_implementation_rating (int 1–10)

Purpose:
High-level clarity and self-honesty

⸻

SECTION 2 — GTD CONTROL CHECK (NEW)

Purpose:
Confirm trust in GTD system, not manage tasks

Fields (boolean checkboxes):
	•	inbox_zero_confirmed
	•	loose_notes_captured
	•	calendar_reviewed
	•	waiting_for_reviewed
	•	next_actions_reviewed
	•	projects_reviewed
	•	someday_reviewed
	•	reference_trusted

Field (short text, optional):
	•	system_breakdown_note

Rules:
	•	No counts
	•	No ratings
	•	All answers are YES/NO
	•	Failure indicates system friction, not personal failure

⸻

⸻

PROCESS RULES
	•	GTD review confirms system trust, not execution detail
	•	Ninja habits measure execution and integrity
	•	Tasks, projects, and calendars live outside this app
	•	This app answers:
	•	“Did I trust my system?”
	•	“Did I execute Ninja?”
	•	“Did inputs create results?”

⸻

FINAL SYSTEM BEHAVIOR

Weekly Operating Review replaces:
	•	Standalone Ninja WMA
	•	Standalone GTD Weekly Review

Outputs:
	•	One structured weekly record
	•	Longitudinal insight
	•	Clean analytics
	•	AI-ready summaries
